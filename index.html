About

Gun control laws are regulations designed to limit the possession, use, and distribution of firearms. This site aims to educate, inform, and inspire action on gun control legislation.

Statistics

Over 45,000 Americans die annually from gun-related incidents. The U.S. has the highest rate of gun ownership globally, with over 120 guns per 100 residents.

Advocacy

Join organizations fighting for change, such as Moms Demand Action and Everytown for Gun Safety. Small actions, like contacting representatives, can create big impacts.
